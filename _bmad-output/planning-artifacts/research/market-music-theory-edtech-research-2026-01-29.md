### Pain Points and Challenges

**ABRSM Exam Anxiety and Fear**

Nervousness is extremely common among ABRSM students, with learners experiencing dry mouth, sweaty hands, shakes, or butterflies in the stomach. Music theory can seem like "such a scary subject" to many students, creating a significant psychological barrier. [_Source: [Song of Songs NY - ABRSM Exam Tips](https://www.songofsongsny.com/post/abrsm-exam-tips-and-tricks-what-to-expect)_]

**Progressive Difficulty and Knowledge Prerequisites**

The ABRSM exam gradually becomes more difficult for each grade, and students must continually progress in their theory lessons as the exam requires extensive prerequisite knowledge. Students often struggle with the cumulative nature of music theory requirements. [_Source: [Song of Songs NY - ABRSM Exam Tips](https://www.songofsongsny.com/post/abrsm-exam-tips-and-tricks-what-to-expect)_]

**Inefficient Practice Patterns**

Students often struggle with breaking practice sessions into smaller, focused segments instead of cramming all areas into one long practice session. Technical flaws need to be identified and corrected early in the preparation process. [_Source: [Song of Songs NY - ABRSM Exam Tips](https://www.songofsongsny.com/post/abrsm-exam-tips-and-tricks-what-to-expect)_]

**Technical and Environmental Challenges (Online Exams)**

Students need to ensure their laptop or computer is compatible with exam software by carrying out system checks. There are specific room requirements including keeping the space free from notes, books, and any representation of a piano keyboard. [_Source: [ABRSM Exam Preparation](https://www.abrsm.org/en-gb/about-our-exams/exam-preparation)_]

### Pricing and Monetisation Insights

**EdTech Pricing Models**

The education market comprises diverse segments with varying willingness to pay, requiring different approaches:

- **Subscription models**: Users pay a recurring fee (monthly or annually) to access premium content, features, and services, providing a predictable and consistent revenue stream
- **Per-user pricing**: The traditional SaaS model charges based on the number of active users
- **Flat fee/Site licensing**: A flat fee regardless of user count, often tiered by institution size, provides budget predictability for schools
- **Freemium**: Users access the core version at no cost, while premium features are available through in-app purchases

[_Source: [Monetizely - Education SaaS Pricing](https://www.getmonetizely.com/articles/the-ultimate-guide-to-education-saas-platform-pricing-testing-finding-your-optimal-price-point)_]

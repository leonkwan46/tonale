## PRINTING SPECIFICATIONS

### Print Format
- **Size:** A4 (210 x 297 mm)
- **Orientation:** Portrait
- **Margins:** 15mm all sides
- **Colour:** Full colour recommended (works in B&W)
- **Paper:** 120gsm glossy or matte
- **Double-sided:** No (single page design)

### Digital Format
- **PDF Export:** High quality (300 DPI)
- **File Size:** Keep under 5MB for email
- **Fonts:** Embed all fonts
- **Images:** High resolution logos/screenshots

### Usage
- **Trade Shows:** Hand out at music education conferences
- **School Visits:** Leave-behind after presentations
- **Email Attachment:** PDF with "View Online" link
- **Landing Page:** Convert to web format
- **Direct Mail:** Send to music school administrators

## DESIGN GUIDELINES

### Colour Palette
- **Primary Blue:** #1E40AF (trust, professionalism)
- **Secondary Purple:** #6D28D9 (creativity, music)
- **Accent Orange:** #F59E0B (calls to action)
- **Background:** White with subtle colour blocks

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Bold, 18-24pt
- **Body Text:** 10-12pt, high readability
- **Emphasis:** Bold or colour for key numbers

### Layout Principles
- **White Space:** Don't overcrowd, let content breathe
- **Hierarchy:** Size and colour indicate importance
- **Scannable:** Use tables, bullets, icons
- **Visual Balance:** Distribute content evenly

### Key Visual Elements
- **Logo:** Top prominent placement
- **Icons:** Consistent style throughout
- **Tables:** Clear borders, alternating row colours
- **Call-to-Action Boxes:** Highlighted background
